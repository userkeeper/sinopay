Compliance

Built so your licence stays clean.

SinoPay is software. You remain the licensed operator. Funds are custodied within your existing operational structure, not ours — meaning regulatory responsibility stays where it belongs, and where you're already authorised.

  • KYC

    Identity verification

    Third-party providers (Sumsub, Veriff) for tier-1 compliance. Configurable thresholds for enhanced due diligence on high-volume guests.

  • AML

    Transaction monitoring

    Pattern detection for structuring, rapid in-out, and unusual velocity. Automated flagging for your compliance officer to review.

  • Data

    Encrypted retention

    AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. 7-year audit-grade record retention, exportable on demand for any regulatory request.

  • Custody

    Operator-held funds

    Guest balances are liabilities on your books, not ours. SinoPay never takes custody — the architecture mirrors your existing cage operations.
